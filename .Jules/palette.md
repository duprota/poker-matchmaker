## 2025-04-16 - Localized aria-labels for icon-only buttons
 **Learning:** The repository is localized in Portuguese, so accessibility improvements such as aria-labels should be provided in Portuguese. Icon-only buttons are highly prevalent throughout the Shadcn UI implementation.
 **Action:** Prioritize adding descriptive, localized `aria-label` attributes to icon-only buttons (`variant="ghost" size="icon"`) to ensure proper accessibility for screen reader users.
